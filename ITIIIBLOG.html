LESSONS

CHAPTER I: WHAT IS COMPUTING?

Computing is a broad field that encompasses the study of algorithms, hardware, software, and data. By studying computing, you can learn how to design, develop, and implement computer systems, as well as how to analyze and solve computational problems.

CHAPTER II: PROFESSIONS & CAREERS IN COMPUTING

Professions and careers in computing encompass a wide range of roles, including software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, and system administration. With the constant evolution of technology, individuals in this field are often involved in solving complex problems, innovating solutions, and contributing to the advancement of digital landscapes.

CHAPTER III: INFORMATION PROCESSING CYCLE

The information processing cycle is like a computer's recipe: it takes in data, works on it, stores it, and then produces a result.
It's the basic way computers handle information for tasks and applications.

CHAPTER IV: INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTER SYSTEMS

An introduction to computer systems involves understanding the components that work together to process information.
This includes hardware like the central processing unit (CPU) and memory, as well as software that enables computers to perform tasks and run programs.

CHAPTER V: BASIC CONCEPT OF COMPUTER SECURITY

Computer security involves protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. Fundamental concepts include authentication, encryption, firewalls, and regular updates to defend against evolving threats and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
